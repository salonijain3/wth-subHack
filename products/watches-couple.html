      Watches for Your Partner and You
      Today when everyone carries a mobile phone that tells them the time, watches do more than just keep time. They are more a fashion statement than just timekeepers. Any outfit is incomplete without a wristwatch. Watches are available in a variety of styles and shapes. There is so much to choose from; you can choose between different shapes of dials, different colours, and different types of straps, and even different mechanisms - digital, analogue or combo of both.

      Different Watch Materials
      Watches come with different types of watchbands. They can be bracelets (composed of metals or precious metals) and straps (composed of plastic, rubber, or leather). Watches made of metals look very elegant and sophisticated. They are available in metals like aluminum, stainless steel, titanium, gold, platinum etc. Sports watches and some casual watches have lightweight, colourful, and sturdy bands made of rubber or plastic. Leather is also a good option; it is sweat absorbent, but pure leather watches are expensive.

      Watches with Different Features
      Watches come with many different features. Waterproof watches and watches with pedometers are handy for someone who is more athletic. Even a chronograph watch, and a watch with a heart monitor are ideal for an athlete. For a person with a busy lifestyle, watches with calendars can be perfect. They display the day, date and year according to the wearer's preference. Some watches also have a moon phase indicator.

      All these different types of watches are available in single pieces for men or women, and they even come in pairs for couples, in the 'his' and 'her' variety. Couple watches make wonderful gifts on weddings, anniversaries, engagements, or any such occasion. They are also a very thoughtful gift for couples to gift each other. These watches can even inscribed with a personal inscription or the name of the wearer, to personalise them.
