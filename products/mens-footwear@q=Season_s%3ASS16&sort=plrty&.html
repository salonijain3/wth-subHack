      A shoe is not merely a fashion accessory; a shoe is an integral part of your body language as the former determines how you walk. Where on one hand well-fitted shoes help one travel miles without experiencing any sort of discomfort, ill-fitted or low quality shoes on the other hand can cause uneasiness and injuries in certain cases. Walk, dance, and do whatever you want with bursting confidence and zero discomfort by choosing the right pair of shoes for men online.


      Men, whether fashion conscious or not, are particular about their shoes. In fact, majority of men believe shoes reflect their personality and thus are concerned about what they wear on their feet.


      Put all your concerns related to men's footwear at rest with the widest assortment of men's shoes, available at Snapdeal.  Be it sport shoes, formal shoes, casual shoes, boots, sandals, slippers, flip flops or loafers for men, you can find it all on Snapdeal.       Snapdeal: Choose Right Shoes for the Right Occasion


      The types of men's shoes available online are quite overwhelming. From sports shoes to boots to loafers, the variety is quite extensive and you need to pick the right shoes to look your best every time.


      Here below we have briefly described each type to help you buy shoes online with ease.


      Formal Shoes: These shoes are perfect for office, workplace, and business meetings. Sharp lines, neat assembling, and top-notch material are a few characteristics that make formal shoes every man's favourite. Whether you want black formal shoes or ones in colours such as brown, maroon, tan, and the likes, you can find it all at Snapdeal in a jiffy.


      Formal shoes are of different types; learn about varied styles so that you can zero on your favourite styles quickly. One of the most common formal shoe types is derby. Derby shoes feature shoelace eyelets, attached to the outside of panels on single-piece uppers. Another popular type is brogue, which is characterised by patterned perforations on the upper. Other common shoe types that you can find under formal shoes are oxfords, monk strap, tassel, and saddles.


      Sports Shoes: Like formal shoes are essential for office, sports shoes are indispensible when playing any kind of game. Sport shoes are designed to provide utmost comfort to the wearer.  Now, when every game is different, how can your shoes be the same! So, the next time you go out to play basketball, wear shoes designed for basketball and not your pair of running shoes. At men's footwear online store on Snapdeal, shoppers can buy sports shoes designed for various sports such as football, cricket, tennis, golf, badminton, hockey, camping, and many more. Don't forget to buy sports gear along with shoes!


      Casual Shoes: As the name suggests, casual shoes are ideal for daily wear and casual occasions. You can wear casual shoes to family get-togethers, a stroll down the lane, or just anywhere of your choice! Though every shopper has specific likes but the most popular types of casual shoes are boat shoes, sneakers, canvas shoes, espadrilles, mules, printed shoes, etc. Take a tour of the men's casual shoes assortment and buy ones that perfectly complement your personality.


      Loafers: In a span of few years, loafers have become the hot favourite. Low and lace-less, these slip-on shoes have become a staple for every guy. If you don't own a pair of loafers yet, it is time to buy loafers for men online at Snapdeal from popular brands like Lee Cooper, Carlton London, Clarks, Alberto Torresi, and Franco Leone. These shoes look perfect with shorts or chinos paired with casual shirts and/or t-shirt.


      Floater Sandals: Floaters or sandals are a must-have in every man's footwear collection. These sandals are lightweight, sturdy, and ensure breathability. Further, floaters can be worn with a variety of outfits, be it casual or ethnic. Majority of floaters sport Velcro closure and upper made of synthetic/faux leather, Croslite or Eva.


      Flip Flops: Flip Flops are lightweight, airy shoes that are usually worn during summertime. Apart from wearing them at home, you can carry a pair of bright hued flip flops to your next beach vacation too. Flip flops come in various designs, prints, and colours and you must buy your favourite pairs right away to add some fun elements to your personal footwear collection.


      Boots: Winters are always incomplete without snug boots! Be it men or women, boots become an instant favourite as soon as the temperature dips. If you are planning to buy sturdy and stylish boots for men, you have landed at the right place. Here, you can find the widest collection of boots from popular brands such as Red Chief, Numero Uno, Woodland, etc. at best prices.


      Punjabi Jutti for Men: Ethnic attires such as sherwani or kurta pyjama look best only when paired with ethnic shoes such as Punjabi jutti. Put an end to your search for a perfect jutti with Snapdeal as here you can find a wide array of ethnic shoes for men and that too at slashed down prices.
