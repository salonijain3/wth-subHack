      Buy Notebooks Online at Best Prices in India at Snapdeal

      It doesn't come as a surprise that the terms 'laptop' and 'notebook' are used interchangeably by users. Both the terms are closely related but there are a few features that differentiate the two from each other. A notebook is a portable personal computer that works like a desktop and comes in a compact size similar to that of a real notebook. When compared to traditional laptops, notebooks weigh less and come in a smaller size. Individuals planning to buy a laptop that works best on the go must check out the wide range of notebooks online at Snapdeal from leading manufacturers like HP, Lenovo, Acer, Apple, ASUS, Dell, Fujitsu, Toshiba, Micromax, MSI, and Xolo.


      Notebooks: Choose Specifications Wisely

      Notebooks are manufactured to target usually entry-level users who prefer mobility over performance. However, it does not mean that notebooks are in anyways inferior to other variants of laptops. Online shoppers can choose the specifications wisely in order to strike a balance between ease of use and performance. One of the toughest decisions is to choose the right processor as it is the brain of a computer and thus largely influences its performance. In case of notebooks, you can choose any one of Intel (Core i3, i5, i7) or AMD processors.


      After processor is the operating system, which again plays a key role in determining the performance level of your laptop. The options available for buyers are Linux, Mac OS X, Chrome, DOS, Windows 7, Windows 8, Windows 8.1 and Windows 10. Other important factors that need to be considered include screen size, memory (RAM), and hard disk capacity. Almost all the notebooks come with integrated graphics, which are sufficient to perform daily computational tasks. However, if you are a hard-core gaming enthusiast then choose a notebook with dedicated graphics. You may also choose between non-touchscreen and touchscreen laptops.


      Snapdeal: Buy Notebooks on Utility Basis

      Understanding technical specifications is not everyone's cup of tea. If all the specifications sound baffling then narrow down your search based on utility and best matching results will be displayed on your screen. For instance, people on a lookout for daily use laptops can choose 'Everyday Use' filter value and best results will be available to choose from. Similarly, 'Entertainment' can be selected for buyers interested in purchasing notebooks for the purpose of entertainment.
