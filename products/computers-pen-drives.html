      The staggering increase in the consumption of digital data has resulted in the invention of a plethora of storage devices, pen drives being one of them. A pen drive, also known as USB Flash Drive, is a portable and rewritable data storage device that consists of flash memory and an integrated USB interface. Pen drives are preferred over other conventional storage devices as they are lightweight, physically smaller, and available in varying capacities like 2 GB, 4 GB, 8 GB, 16 GB, 32 GB, 64 GB, 128 GB, etc. Browse through the wide collection of pen drives and enjoy a hassle free pen drive online shopping experience on Snapdeal as you can narrow down your search according to capacity, price, brand, interface, and type.


      What makes Pen Drive a good storage device?


      There are multiple reasons why pen drives are the preferred choice of users when it comes to buy a reliable storage device. Firstly, a pen drive is sleek and lightweight and thus easy to carry along. Whether you choose a 16 GB or 32 GB pen drive, the size will be small enough to carry your pen drive in your handbag, wallet or pocket. Since flash drives are primarily used for storing personal and important information, you can use various encryption methods to keep the information secure. Buy a special set of security pen drives at Snapdeal to protect your information from being stolen and misused. Pen drives can store digital data of almost every kind including documents, images, audio clips, videos, and HD movies. Further, they can be used on all platforms like Mac, Windows, Chrome, etc. without requiring any special installation software.

      Keep your collection of storage devices up-to-date with OTG Pen Drives. Most of the OTG pen drives are compatible with both USB 2.0 and USB 3.0 interfaces. Thanks to these storage devices, you can establish a communication link between USB devices with quite an ease.
