      Dive Into a World of Fun

      All water activities like, swimming, diving, surfing, snorkelling, etc. are so much fun to do when the warm weather arrives. However, no sport is complete without the right gear and accessories. The right equipment can enhance the entire experience.

      Essential Equipment for Water Sports

      A swimsuit or a pair of swimming shorts are essential for swimming. A good swimming costume should be tight but comfortable. Buy a swimsuit you're comfortable in, be it a one-piece or a two-piece. Swimming goggles are an equally essential piece of equipment. They keep your eyes clear in the water for visibility, and also protect them from chlorinated pool water. If the goggles leak water into your eyes, it is because they are not of a good fit. Goggles should be a snug fit so that the water does not leak into the eyes. Gaskets are the part of the goggles around the eye socket and come in contact with your face. Gaskets should be made of silicone, rubber, or foam, to be comfortable. Light blue or clear lenses are good for indoor swimming and darker blues for outdoor to reduce surface glare from water. Smoke coloured lenses are a good all-purpose option. Swimming caps are meant to protect your hair from chlorine in swimming pools, and also keep hair out of the face. If you are a competitive swimmer, then choose a swimming cap made of silicone. It is wrinkle-resistant and reduces drag to improve performance during a race. Recreational swimmers can buy less-expensive latex swimming caps, or a more expensive and durable silicone cap.

      How to Care for Your Swimming Gear

      If you want to get the most out of your swimming gear, then you must care for it. Always rinse your swimwear in cold water after swimming; otherwise the swimsuit loses its elasticity. After using you swimming cap, rinse it well under fresh water and let it air dry. Latex walls tend to stick together; so after drying, it is a good idea to coat the inside with talcum powder, before putting them away in your swimming kit.
