      Shop Online for Computer Accessories
      Of all computer accessories, an extension cord is one of the important ones. A computer has a lot of parts that require separate sources of power and this gives rise to the need for multiple power outlets. Since more than two or three adjacent plug points are not always available, it is feasible to opt for extension cords. Another major advantage of these extension cords is that they also protect the monitor and the CPU from voltage fluctuations. There are 3, 4, 6 and even 8 socket extension cords available at Snapdeal, so opt for the one that is compatible with your computer. You can also take your pick from international plugs, power AC adapters and more. All these products are required to power your computer.       For the Desktop and the Laptop
      Computer accessories are useful for both desktop computers and laptops. Products like USB hubs that allow multiple USB devices to connect to your desktop or laptop, are convenient. Cleaning kits are also available here and they help you to maintain your device. A card reader is another important accessory to order. The HDMI cable price range is also quite affordable and thus you can pick up one of those too.
