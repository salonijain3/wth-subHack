      Indian civilization is more than five thousand years old and very rich in culture. Influence of ethnicity, tradition, dynasties and migrated culture from rest of the globe made it incredible.  This reflects in Indian music, food, art and very much in clothing. The diversity and richness of clothing is visible not only in women but also in mens ethnic wear. If kurta pajama for men is casual regular wear, Sherwani for men has a royal fashion statement. Kurta pyjama for men is a popular dressing at Indian subcontinent. The straight cut kurta is a knee length loose shirt that you generally wear with dhoti or pyjama. At Snapdeal, you will find varieties of kurtas that are found in different part of India like kali kurta, Bhopali, Hydrabadi, Lakhnowi or dogri kurtas.

      Shop Online For Mens Ethnic Wear

      Kurtas for men are available in variety of fabrics and the choice depends on personal preferences. Popular fabrics are silk, cotton, khadi, linen, jacquard etc. Unlike kurta, Sherwani for men is comparatively formal and is a part of the royal dressing code. It is a below knee length long coat that can be worn on top of churidar pyjamas. Sherwani is generally worn during Indian marriages or in traditional occasions. Turban or dupatta are additional accessories with Sherwani. Products of popular brands like Raymond Ethnix, Fabindia and Manyavar are available at Snapdeal.

      For the New Groom

      A groom’s Sherwani is popular in cream, light ivory or gold as far as the colour is concerned. They come in ethnic design, embroidery or have zari, semi-precious stones, beads and buttons attached. Sherwani is available in various fabrics like silk, brocade, banarasi, etc. and also in numerous designs with variety of stitches like kantha, pashmina or chickan. Even some of the sherwanis can come with gold or precious stones.
