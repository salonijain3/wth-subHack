      How to Take Care of Silk Sarees?


      Needless to say, glistening silk sarees are liked by one and all. However, if you want your beautiful silk saree to remain new and shiny always, you need to take proper care of it. Here below are mentioned a few points that should be followed to keep your silk sari new always.


      • Do not use harsh chemicals while washing a silk sari as it may result in loss of shine and affects the lifespan of saree. Instead, wash your silk saree separately with a mild detergent.
      • While washing, do not use a brush as it may spoil zari on silk saree.
      • Silk sarees must always be wrapped in muslin cloth before putting storing in the wardrobe.
      • If you are not going to wear a silk saree for long, you must ensure to unfold and change the folding at regular intervals.
