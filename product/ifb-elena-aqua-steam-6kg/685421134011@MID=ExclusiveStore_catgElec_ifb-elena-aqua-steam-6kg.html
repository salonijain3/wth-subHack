      Description

      The Elena Aqua Steam 6Kg 1000 RPM is a front loading washing machine which is designed to offer an optimal washing experience while being gentle on clothes. The new age design helps save water as well as energy, thus saving you money. The Elena Aqua Steam even dries clothes quick and easy. The complete electronic controls are easy to use and can be programmed as per your requirement.

      If persistent stains bother you, then Elena Aqua Steam is just for you! With advanced features such as Aqua Energie and unique Steam Wash System, this washing genius turns your clothes out impeccably. Elena Aqua Steam’s Refresh Program keeps your clothes odour and wrinkle free.

      Steam Wash System

      Steam Wash System feature turns cold water into steam with the help of a heater. Steam molecules being smaller than water, seep deeper into the fabric and stir out even the tiniest dirt particles. As a result, clothes come out smoother, softer and fresher.

       

      Aqua Energie

      A special built-in aqua filter treats hard water improving the life and performance of your machine. It breaks the bi-carbonates in the water and reduces them to crystals. These crystals are microscopically small and flow with the water instead of forming crusts. The filtered water is soft and facilitates wetting of clothes. The detergent enzymes dissolve in water giving your clothes a better wash.

      2D Shower System

      It adds dynamic movement to the water and ensures that you get a cleaner and fresher wash for your clothes anytime. A special water system with nozzles circulates the water 360° in the drum. The detergent dissolves 100% and penetrates the clothes deeper for a cleaner wash. During rinsing, fresh water penetrates deep into the clothes to wash off all traces of detergent. The clothes come out cleaner and fresher.

      Laundry Add

      Missed out on your favourite shirt? This feature lets you add laundry even after wash cycle has started.

      Crescent Moon Drum

      The crescent moon shaped pattern on the inner drum protects clothes during the wash. Water curves up in a swoosh pattern and prevents the clothes from rubbing against the wall of the steel drum. Clothes get a gentle yet thorough cleaning.

      Protective Rat Mesh

      An additional cover at the bottom of the machine protects against possible damage caused by rodents.

      Foam Control System

      It automatically senses the generation of foam and activates a dilution system to dissolve the excessive foam. It helps maintain the wash quality.
