      Description

      Overview

      Get skin-friendly trimming results time after time with Philips BT 990 Trimmer. It comes with sharp blades that cut hairs neatly providing you a clean trim.  The blade tips of this trimmer are rounded which help in preventing irritation and itchiness. With four in-built comb settings, you can create stylish stubble easily without having to visit a salon. This Philips trimmer runs on two AA batteries that give you up to two months of usage. It is easy to clean and store. Buy BT 990 trimmer by Philips at best price from Snapdeal and get 2 years Philips India warranty on the product. 

      Skin Friendly

      Philips BT 990 Trimmer has stainless steel blades. The sharp blades of this trimmer ensure a clean performance. The tips of these blades are rounded for smooth skin contact. Its non-scratching teeth prevent any kind of irritation that may occur after trimming is done. It is extremely easy and comfortable to use.

      4 Built-In Length Settings

      You can slide the comb up to the desired length setting for achieving the beard style you desire. The length settings can be adjusted from 1.5 mm to 7 mm. Without the comb, you can trim your beard at 0.5 mm length. There is a flexing guide comb that follows every curve of your face for getting a precise and even trim.

      Easy To Clean

      Philips BT 990 Trimmer is extremely easy to clean. It is a hassle-free appliance that provides long lasting performance. You simply have to pop open the head of the trimmer and clean it with the brush provided to sweep off the loose hairs.

      Built to Last

      This Philips grooming appliance is made of durable material that lasts for a long period of time. The stainless steel blades provide effective performance. The pop open head of this trimmer makes it more convenient to store and clean. You will never have to oil this appliance ever. It will work efficiently for years without requiring any professional maintenance. 

      Runs on AA Batteries

      The feature that makes Philips BT 990 Trimmer highly user-friendly is its portability. It is light in weight & easy to store in a pouch or bag. Moreover, you do not require to plug it in an electrical socket for functioning. Simply insert two AA batteries and the trimmer will deliver up to 120 minutes of cordless power. One set of batteries can deliver up to 2 months of usage.
