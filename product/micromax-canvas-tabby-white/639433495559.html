      Creativity Apps

      Entertainment Apps

      17.78 cm (7) TFT Display

      Equipped with a large 17.78 cm (7) TFT display, the Micromax canvas Tabby gives you an enthralling viewing experience. With 1024 x 600 pixels and 242K colour depth, this excellent tablet delivers crystal clear viewing experience. It is great for watching videos, playing games, reading as well as texting.

      Android KitKat Operating System (OS) with Kids Mode

      The Micromax canvas tabby comes with the latest Android KitKat operating system (OS) with kids’ mode. It facilitates you to download innumerable kids’ friendly apps in the device, watch graphic intensive games and enjoy exciting games. 

      1.3 GHz Dual Core Processor

      Loaded with the powerful 1.3 GHz dual core processor, the Canvas tabby loads your web pages faster and enables you to access your apps instantly. 1 GB of RAM speeds up the processing power of the phone and delivers a fast and responsive navigation experience.

      2 MP Rear Camera with Flash and VGA Front Camera

      The Canvas tabby also enables you to capture those precious moments of life forever with the 2 MP rear camera of the tablet. It comes with a flash support that helps you click clear and bright pictures even in dim or low light situations. Canvas tabby by Micromax also helps you to record videos at 1920x1080 pixel resolution. The VGA front camera is great for video conferencing through third party apps such as Skype. 

      3 Distinct child Profiles

      With Micromax Canvas Tabby, you can create 3 distinct child profiles and personalize their content accordingly. Every child can access the age-appropriate content which will help them to sharpen their skills and explore their imagination to the fullest with parents observation and guidance.

      Parental Control

      Canvas tabby gives you the clear insights of your kid’s activities along with the areas of interest. It also enables you to set a time limit on this tablet on particular days or for completing a specific task. Additionally, it also facilitates you to block or add content and change the settings of the device online. With Micromax canvas Tabby by your side, you never have to worry about your child getting involved into any inappropriate online content.

      Parent Dashboard

      The parent dashboard facilitates you to fully customize and access the Canvas Tabby according to your priorities.  It facilitates you to customize the kids’ profile, filter content, set time limits, view reports, change phone settings all with a touch of the screen.

      Content Filter

      The content filter enables you to add or block a particular content from the Tab. This excellent feature assures you that your child is using the Canvas tabby responsibly. You can add or delete videos, apps or app category and more through the content filter feature of the tabby to prevent your child from landing into any inappropriate online content.

      Time Manager

      With the time manager feature you can set timer for each app category such as education, creativity, entertainment and more. You can also limit the time range for certain apps so that your child uses it appropriately.  

      Memory

      Manage your multimedia files efficiently with the Canvas Tabby by Micromax. It offers an incredible 8 GB of internal memory that can also be expanded up to 32 GB by using a microSD card.

      Tabby Bumper Case

      Encased in a robust bumper case, the Micromax Canvas Tabby enables your child to use the tablet the way they want. This strong bumper case protects the tablet from dents and damages and also works as a stand. It can even double up as a carry case with a handle. Moreover, the matte finish rubber feel casing makes it more durable.

      Dual Mode for Parents and Kids

      You can use the Micromax Canvas Tabby with dual mode for parents and kids. While using this tablet in the parent-use mode, it serves as a fully-functional tablet with all the high-end features. Once, you activate it for your kids, it serves as the best educational tool to enhance their learning in a fun way.

      3200 mAh Marathon Non-removable Battery

      Fuelled by a powerful 3200 mAh marathon non-removable battery, the Micromax canvas tabby keep your children entertained for a longer time.  From playing exciting games to reading stories, this excellent tab encourages your child to explore new and creative ways of learning.  It delivers a talk time of up to 11.5 hours, standby time of 158 hours, internet browsing time of 3.5 hours and video playback time of 6 hours.

It offers multiple connectivity options such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, GPS and more.  You can transfer files via Bluetooth 2.1 connectivity of the tablet.
