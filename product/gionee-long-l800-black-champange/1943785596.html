      Description

      Overview

      Feel the difference in Feature Phone category with Gionee Long L800 Black Champange. The Dual SIM phone has a TFT screen size of 6.6 CM and has a resolution of QVGA, 320 x 240 Pixels for superior picture quality. The 1.3 MP camera gives you the opportunity to grab and store the world around you without giving it a miss. Moreover, The 16 MB Internal Memory can also be extended upto 16 GB with a Micro SD card, if you need to store many files of your need. The 3000 mAh Li-Polymer Battery keeps fuelling the talktime until 20 Hrs and upto 675 Hrs while on Standby Mode, in addition it allows you to use some cool pre loaded apps such as Facebook, Twitter, Yahoo Messenger, Cricket, Yahoo without worrying about the battery.

       

      Dual SIM Support

      The phone comes with GSM Dual SIM Slots (Dual Standby Facility) to facilitate your needs of having multiple of numbers for various purposes. It easily operates on both the SIMS and enables you to comfortable call and text while switching from one SIM to another or while surfing on the internet.

       

      Screen Size

      The TFT screen size is 6.6 CM which has a resolution of QVGA, 320 x 240 Pixels. It comfortable fulfils the visual needs of a viewer. 

       

      Camera

      Gionee Long L 800 comes equipped with 1.3 MP Rear Camera with fixed focus facility. It also enables you to record videos at 8 FPS and click photos at your convenience.

      Connectivity Options

      It gets connected with the internet and with other devices with WAP, Opera Mini, WAP 2.0, GPRS, Edge, Audio Jack-3.5 MM, Bluetooth, Email, USB features.

       

      Memory Capacity

      The internal memory of the phone is 16 MB and caters to your basic needs but this can be extended upto 16 GB with the help of a Micro SD Card. 

       

      Battery Performance

      The stunning Gionee Long L800 is a remarkable phone in terms of its battery. It can really charge your needs to an unexpected level. The 3000 mAh Li-Polymer Battery gives talktime of 20 Hrs on 2G and 675 Hrs of Stand-By Time and guides you out of any battery crisis situation.

       

      Additional Features

      The Phone is a club of some real cool features such as Preinstalled Browser: Opera Mini, WAP 2.0; Email, Facebook, Twitter, Yahoo Messenger, Cricket, Yahoo FM Radio etc.

       

      Colour Variants

      As the name itself signifies, the handset comes in l Elegant Black Champange Colour and looks stunning in hand.
