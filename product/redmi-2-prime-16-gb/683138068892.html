      Description

      Slim, lightweight and extremely user-friendly, the all-new Mi Redmi 2 smartphone will certainly, give you numerous reasons to make you fall in love with it.  Equipped with a bright HD display, the Mi smartphone showcases every image with maximum clarity and true to life colours. Power-packed with the latest processor along with the advanced operating system, this ultimate smartphone thrills you with seamless multitasking and smooth operating experience. Click beautiful pictures, play graphic intensive games, watch high definition videos, take amazing screenshots and browse web with the highly advanced smartphone brought to you by Mi. Additionally, the powerful battery performance of the smartphone always keeps you on the go. Get ready to take your multimedia experience a notch higher with Mi smartphone.  You can buy Mi Redmi 2 smartphone at best price along with 1 year Mi India warranty only at Snapdeal.

      Oh snap, 64-bit! Snapdragon 410 quad-core 1.2-GHz

      With 50% more processing power than its predecessor, Cortex A7, the Cortex-A53 processor on Qualcomm's Snapdragon 410 guarantees a fluid experience even for the heaviest of users. Its energy efficient Hexagon DSP core maximises work per CPU clock cycle for long lasting battery life and wait for it... 64-bit computing! It's faster and more powerful than 32-bit processors, and allows for more efficient processing and memory use. It also facilitates you to play 3D games with Adreno 306 GPU. Additionally, 2 GB of RAM maximizes the overall performance of the smartphone and amazes you with a smooth navigation experience like never before.

      4G Dual SIM. Up to 150Mbps download speeds

      Let your worlds collide with 4G dual SIM, dual standby. You won't have to manually switch SIM cards around with 4G support on both slots. Intelligent dual SIM features allow you to manage calls and messages easily, according to preferred network and data settings for each card. For instance, Redmi 2 allows you to create custom SIM labels so you don’t get mixed up. It also automatically selects which SIM to use when you call or text someone based on history. It offers an exceptional 150 Mbps download speed and has 4G Support on both slots. You can conveniently use Micro SIMs in the device.

      Sharp/AUO/EBBG 4.7" IPS fully laminated display

      Soak in the view with 1280x720 HD resolution at 312 pixels per inch (PPI). 178° in-plane switching (IPS) technology delivers stunning color consistency from almost any angle. We've also designed a fully laminated display which allows pixels to sit close to the glass, so colors pop right off the screen and are less affected by glare. All this is protected with a highly shatter and scratch-resistant AGC Dragontrail glass that is known for being incredibly strong despite being very thin and light.

       8MP BSI rear camera 5-element lens

      Take sharp, clear images. The 8MP camera uses backside illumination (BSI) for great low-light performance at night, while infrared sensor protection improves image quality in sunlight. Shoot in HDR, panorama, fisheye, use voice commands, and more. It comes with Autofocus mechanism along with LED Flash support that facilitates you to highly focussed pictures even in low light shooting conditions. It supports 1080p Full HD Video recording for capturing those fleeting moments of life forever.

      Excellent for Selfies

      Photoshop is a thing of past! Now, look young and gorgeous every time with the new Mi Redmi 2 smartphone that comes with an advanced ‘Beautify App’. It intelligently guesses your age and gender and applies 36 smart filters to enhance your appearance. Get ready to flaunt your beautiful selfies on social network sites with Mi. Moreover, you can also record high definition (720p) videos with the front camera of the smartphone. Selfies look great even without photo editing.

      Thinner & Lighter

      Comfort without compromise. We've kept the same 4.7" display but designed Redmi 2 to be lighter at 133g and thinner at 9.4mm. It also features rounded corners and a textured back that feels great in your grip.

      Matte matters

      Finely textured and matte-sprayed, the back cover feels great against skin. It is also fingerprint and grease-resistant.
       

      Even better inside - MIUI 6

      Discover new functions, shortcuts, customizations and animations embedded within MIUI 6. Each swipe, pinch and flick is co-developed and upgraded regularly, based on improvement suggestions from some 100 million active MIUI users worldwide. Download innumerable applications, discover new functions and shortcuts with the power of the advanced MIUI based on Android 4.4 Operating System (OS). With this advanced operating system you can quickly access the files and apps stored in the smartphone along with a prioritised contact list. MIUI 6 is the real USP of the device. It offers exciting new features such as:

      ·         Security Centre:  Protects the device from Virus attacks, manage software and optimizes Battery

      ·         Built-in Music Apps

      ·         Calendar

      ·         Mi Cloud (10GB of storage and 6 layers of data encryption)

      2200 mAh Battery

      Powered by the high performance 2200 mAh battery, the new Mi Redmi 2 smartphone allows you to easily accomplish your important tasks without any interruption. Dive into the world of unlimited entertainment with the Mi smartphone that comes with the latest Quick charge 1.0 technology that speeds up the charging time by 20%. Stay connected with your friends 24 x7, browse web, play entertaining games and listen to music without worrying about the battery getting drained out.

      16 GB Internal Memory and 32 GB Expandable Memory

      From your favourite videos to the latest songs, store anything that you like in the Mi smartphone that offers an excellent 16 GB of internal memory. Moreover, you can expand the memory of the device up to 32 GB by using a microSD card.

      Dual mic Noise Reduction

      Redmi 2 incorporates two noise-cancellation microphones that offers enhanced signal-to-noise ratio than traditional microphones. It enables you to hear crystal clear sound even when you are in a noisiest environment.

      Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Enabled

      Share your pictures on Facebook, watch movies online, play entertaining games online and browse web at blazing fast speed with the Wi-Fi connectivity of the smartphone. Simply, connect the Mi Redmi 2 smartphone with the available Wi-Fi network and enjoy seamless internet services at superfast speed. Additionally, the Bluetooth connectivity of the smartphone allows you to share your data wirelessly to other Bluetooth enabled devices. Other connectivity options include:

      ·         Wi-Fi Hotspot

      FM Radio

      JUST ONE WORD "FANTASTIC"

      As The Name Itself Say's It Is of Really Prime TYPE Pick up the Redmi 2 Prime and you will instantly notice how light the phone is and how slippery its removable plastic back is. The 8MP F/2.2 camera protrudes ever so slightly, but its not big enough to cause the phone to wobble when it is lying flat on a table. Prying open the back cover will give you access to the removable 2200mAh battery, which comes in Xiaomis trademarked deep orange color. Take out the battery and you will gain access to the two microSIM card slots and the microSD card slot of the handset. The power and the volume button are located on the right edge of the Redmi 2 Prime and are actually a part of the back cover itself. Ideally, I would have preferred Xiaomi to place the volume and power buttons on the opposite edges of the phone. Since the phone comes with a 4.7-inch display, and I have a large palm, I kept hitting the volume button every time I took the phone out from my pocket. This problem, thankfully, can be bypassed by enabling Volume Wake in MIUI. The buttons offer decent tactile feedback, though the power button does feel a bit squishy. Now Coming To Display Of THIS DEVICE The Redmi 2 Prime comes with a 4.7-inch 720p IPS display a pretty standard fare in budget smartphones available in this range. Head on, the display is good enough for day-to-day usage with more than ample brightness levels, contrast ratio, and touch response. Make no mistake though; the display is not going to win any awards for color accuracy or contrast levels, but it is more than good enough to get the job done. Even when you look at the phone from sideways or just about any other angle, the display remains visible, though with a yellowish tint to it. The display does sit as close to the glass as it does on the Mi 4i, but considering that the latter is priced at 2x its retail price, this trade off is not something that anyone using the Prime NOW TAKING ITS ITS CORE THAT IS ITS MIUI Like every other device from Xiaomi, the Redmi 2 Prime runs on MIUI 6, which is based on Android 4.4 KitKat. The amount of customisation options offered in MIUI are mind boggling, and can be overwhelming for a first time smartphone user. For users who love customisation though, MIUI will come as a breath of fresh air. The skin allows you to tweak almost every aspect of the UI according to your liking, and its powerful theme engine makes sure that you can spice up things, in case you get bored with the stock look. My major gripe with the Redmi 2 Primes software, is that it still runs on KitKat a version of Android that was released nearly two years ago. It does not matter if MIUI offers an experience that is completely unrelated to what Android version it runs on, as apps that take advantage of the new APIs of Lollipop are not going to run on it. The Redmi 2 Prime is powered by a Snapdragon 410 chipset, which is a pretty standard fare for devices in the same price range. However, the device comes with 2GB RAM still rare in sub-$150 Android devices that gives it an edge over its competition. Thanks to the extra RAM, the Prime is a better multitasker than its non-Prime version. as it is able to keep apps for much longer in its memory. The extra gig of RAM also helps the handset while playing games, as heavy games like Riptide GP2 lag less when compared to the Moto E or even the Redmi 2. Thats not to say that the Prime is able to handle heavy games without any kind of stuttering though; there is still plenty of jerks and frame drops while playing games like Asphalt 8, which does ruin the overall experience of playing them. Casual games like Flow, Threes! and Dots run just fine on the handset, though. NOW TAKING THE MOST IMPORTANT ONE ITS CAMERA Many of you want to know about its camera . The Redmi 2 Prime comes with an 8MP shooter with an F/2.2 aperture. If that was impressive enough, the handset is also capable of recording Full HD videos, which was almost an unheard of feature in devices available in this price range just six months ago. The photos shot from the Redmi 2 Prime have a typical Xiaomi-feel to them. Colors and saturation are both increased to add a pop effect to the photos. The low-light performance of the camera is disappointing, as the sensor fails to suck in a decent amount of light or capture any meaningful details.but overall you can consider it best in the price range of 10k BATTERY LIFE The Redmi 2 Prime comes with a 2200mAh removable battery, which is easily able to power the device through a day of heavy usage. I constantly managed to get more than 5 hours of screen-on time on the Prime over a period of 1.5 days on 3G/4G network. On light usage, I was able to make the phone last through one whole weekend plus the Monday that followed. Keep in mind that this was with 2 Gmail accounts syncing in the background and a messaging clients like WhatsApp, Hangouts and Telegram running in the background. Final Verdict---- JUST GO FOR IT
