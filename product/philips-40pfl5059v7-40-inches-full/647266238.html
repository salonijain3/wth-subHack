      Description

      Convert your home into a movie hall with the crystal clear and loud audio experience of Philips 40PFL5059/V7 LED TV. With 16 W RMS power, this TV is capable to produce highly immersive audio performance.

       

      It is a 102 cm, 5000 Series full HD LED TV which aims at delivering great picture quality. It has a display resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. Along with which you will see the following features to enhance the overall picture quality.

      • 3/2 - 2/2 motion pull down
      • 3D Combfilter
      • Digital Noise Reduction
      • Dynamic contrast
      • Pixel precise HD

       

      The Stereo Sound System has two major sound enhancement features: Auto Volume Leveller and Incredible Surround. Both these features add to the credibility and beauty of the audio performance of this TV.

       

      You can now play your favourite shows, movies, videos and pictures with the help of the USB drive. Following are the content file extensions supported by this TV:

      • .avi
      • .mkv
      • .mov
      • .mp4
      • .mpg
      • .mpeg
      • .wmv
      • Witness Excellent Motion Sharpness With 240Hz Perfect Motion Rate

      Philips uses 240 Hz Perfect Motion Rate to create extreme motion sharpness with clear and vibrant on screen display images. This is especially useful in fast action movies. Perfect Motion Rate is commonly known as PMR which shows the joint visible effect of the fastest panel refresh rate, HD Natural Motion and unique processing formula offering extraordinary motion sharpness.

      • Upgrade Your Audio Enjoyment With Incredible Surround

      Philips 40PFL5059/V7 offers the feature of Incredible Surround which is basically an audio technology developed by Philips. This technology expands the sound field to provide you a mesmerising audio environment. It uses the latest electronic phase shifting to produce sound. Incredible Surround blends the sounds from left and right. This mix leads to expansion of virtual distance between the two speakers. This inclusion of this wider spread aims at enhancing the stereo effect and thus creates a more realistic sound dimension.

      • 16 Watt RMS For Brilliant Sound Experience At Home

      With Philips 40PFL5059/V7 enjoy your favourite shows in a new way. It has the all-powerful 16 W RMS (2 x 8 W RMS) amplifiers which are designed to produce true to life sound stage. This sound stage is clear and expansive to deliver immersive audio experience for home viewing.

      • 2 HDMI Inputs For Seamless Connectivity Between The Device and The TV

      Philips 40PFL5059/V7 can play content via HDMI inputs. HDMI stands for High Definition Multimedia Interface which develops an uncompressed digital RGB connection directly from the source to the screen offering incredible picture quality. HDMI uses HDCP copy protection. The 2 HDMI inputs are present at the back of the TV and you can easily connect multiple HD sources to the TV for example, HD set top box, Blu-ray player, Game Console and Digital Camcorder.

      • Enjoy Better Depth and Clarity With Full HD LCD Display

      Philips 40PFL5059/V7 has a Full HD screen with a widescreen resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. The mentioned resolution is considered to be the highest resolution to produce clear picture and HD content via HD sources. This full HD Display is futuristic as it has been developed to support 1080p signals from all sources. It includes the latest devices like Blu-ray and advanced HD game consoles. Witness wonderful flicker-free pictures with progressive scan along with which you will also get incredible brightness and splendid colours.

      • Play Your Preferred Video, Photos and Music From Your Stored Content in The USB

      Philips 40PFL5059/V7 allows you to play digital content from your USB drive. Simply plug the USB in the available slot to play the content. The slot is available at the side of the TV. Access multimedia content using the on screen content browser. The USB connector lets you access to jpeg photos, mp3 music and video files.
