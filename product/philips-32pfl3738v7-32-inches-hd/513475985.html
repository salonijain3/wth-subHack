      Description

      Bring home the world of superb entertainment with Philips 32PFL3738 LED TV. Enjoy watching your favourite shows with greater level of brightness and clarity.

       

      It is a 7000 Series LED which has the feature of Perfect Pixel HD which is a picture engine to enhance your viewing experience. Also, it improves the 3D performance, bringing life like images to your living room.

       

      Perfect Pixel HD Engine for unrivalled sharpness and clarity

      Perfect Pixel HD Engine by Philips LED TV offers incredibly clear picture quality. The technology enhances each pixel of the picture to match the surrounding pixels in a better way. This process delivers more realistic picture. It is basically a unique combination of sharpness, true to life detail, vibrant colours, superior contrast and smooth natural motion for enhanced picture quality.

      Experience Ultra Smooth Motion With HD Natural Motion

      Philips has the feature of HD Natural Motion that aims at minimising the juddering effect. This effect is commonly seen in movie based picture content. HD Natural Motion detects the unnecessary motion and corrects the juddering movements in both broadcast and recorded movie material. The recorded movie material might include DVDs and Blu-ray Discs.

      Digital Entertainment By Philips HDTV Is Future Proof

      Philips 32PFL3738 has futuristic technology. It offers you to enjoy and experience premium picture quality with over 4.4 trillion colours and enhanced sharpness, improved clarity and better contrast. It lets your TV to accept digital video directly from the satellite. This makes the use of a set-top box completely pointless.

       

      Watch The Most Comfortable 3D With Easy 3D

      Philips LED TV has the feature of Easy 3D. It is considered to be the most comfortable 3D to watch. It is a passive 3D technology that makes sure that you enjoy long hours of comfortable 3D watching with flicker-free and low-ghosting 3D performance. The best part about this 3D technology is that the 3D glasses are highly light in weight and have a smart look. These glasses do not need batteries to work. Convert any 2D content in to a flawless 3D with just a push of a button and enjoy 3D viewing at the comfort of your living room with friends.

      Push A Button To Enjoy Any 2D Content In Impeccable 3D Quality

      Philips 32PFL3738 LED TV gives you the option to watch your entire movie collection in flawless 3D. It includes the old black-and-white classics as well. Philips considerably offers real-time pictures with high-quality conversion a 2D content to 3D content at the push of a button. Along with which it also makes sure that the subtitles are shown at a continuous depth. Also, it ensures that there are no such errors where background appears in the foreground.

      Experience An All New Way Of 3D Depth Adjustment

      With Philips 32PFL3738 LED TV you can now customise the 3D depth level according to your convenience and personal liking. This is a unique feature by Philips where you can adjust the 3D depth of the image on the screen making it more comfortable for your eyes.

      Watch Blur Free On Screen Pictures With 120 Hz Perfect Motion Rate

      Philips 32PFL3738 has the capacity of 120Hz Perfect Motion Rate. Perfect Motion Rate is also known as PMR which offers picture with incredible motion sharpness, delivering crystal clear and vibrant images. This is especially meant for the fast paced action movies, where motion blur has been noticed. Enjoy watching your favourite action packed film with reduced motion blur with this amazing feature by Philips.  It is a combined visible effect of the fastest panel refresh rate, HD Natural Motion and unique processing formula.

       

      16 W (2x 8 W) RMS Power Of The Speakers For Immersive Audio

      Philips 32PFL3738 offers you a room filling experience of engaging music. Feel every beat of the song and enjoy your favourite songs with your friends and family. This immersive music is a result of the powerful 16 W RMS (2 x 8 W RMS) amplifiers. These amplifiers are capable of creating a true to life sound stage. Enjoy rich entertainment with clear and expansive sound stage at the comfort of your home.

       

      Control All The Actions With A Single Remote Controller

      Now you can watch, play, change channels, control your set top box, and much more with a single remote controller. Philips 32PFL3738 allows you to watch TV in a convenient way. This eradicates the use of additional set-top box.

       

      Play Video, Photo and Music With USB Connectivity

      Philips 32PFL3738 LED TV is capable of playing digital media files directly from the memory drive. The USB connector allows you to play jpeg photos, mp3 music and video files with the aid of all USB drives. To make this work simply plug the USB into the available USB slot that is present at the side of the TV. Take use of the easy on screen content browser to access multimedia content. Watch your favourite movies, photos and music files and relive old memories!
