      Description

       

       

      People scouting for aesthetically appealing and highly functional air conditioner to fit into their window have a perfect solution draped in the form of <a href=" http://www.snapdeal.com/brand/hitachi/appliances">Hitachi</a>  1 Ton 5 Star Kaze Plus RAW511KUD Window Air Conditioner. Identified as a super-efficient AC, it is now a matter of a simple click on Snapdeal before one becomes a proud owner of this wondrous home cooling product, much to the appeal of the entire family. Hence it a wise move to purchase the Hitachi 1 Ton 5 Star Kaze Plus RAW511KUD Window Air Conditioner and benefit from a 1 year comprehensive warranty along with a 4 year warranty on its compressor.

      AC Type

      As the name specifies, this is a Window AC sporting an elegant white colour which can gel with the tapestry and décor of a living room or a bed room. Demonstrating a lower pull down time, this can be called a powerful air conditioner functioning to the utmost satisfaction of its users. Speaking of the durability of this make of AC, successful passing through 43 stringent quality control tests speaks volumes.

      Power Consumption

      Stamped with a 5 Star Rating, the Hitachi 1 Ton 5 Star Kaze Plus RAW511KUD Window Air Conditioner becomes a perfect cooling product to be termed as an energy efficient air conditioner. Requiring an input voltage of 230 V running on a single phase, its power supply frequency measures 50 Hz. Equipped with an Auto Power Save Mode, this AC has the support of Auto Fan Speed towards cutting down on electricity bills.

      Technology Used

      This brand and make of air conditioners makes the most of the Auto Climate Technology (ACT), much to the comfort of inmates. Additionally, another feature that attracts the attention of users is the auto power save mode which tops the list of benefits. The Hitachi 1 Ton 5 Star Kaze Plus RAW511KUD Window Air Conditioner is proud of its Auto Mode and Cool Mode along with the supporting of a Dry Mode.

      Hassle Free Operation

      Supported by an auto-restart function, this AC delivers to its optimum capacity through its 3 way speed setting. These variations of speeds which can be adjusted easily make it an easy to operate air conditioner.

      Remote and Front Panel Display

      Sporting an LED front panel display, the Hitachi 1 Ton 5 Star Kaze Plus RAW511KUD Window Air Conditioner is proud of a big wireless LCD remote control. An additional benefit comes draped in the form of a remote backlight. This AC has the advantage of a Filter Clean Indicator.

      Auto Swing and Louver Movement

      Incorporating the up and down swinging motion of the air circulating flap, this comes as a perfect AC through the use of its powerful mode. Additionally, its air circulation outwards measures 400 CFM. Supported by Low Derating which provides 10% additional cooling during extreme summers, the Hitachi 1 Ton 5 Star Kaze Plus RAW511KUD Window Air Conditioner has another advantage coming in the form of pre-coated aluminium fins which help in the uniform circulation of fresh and pure air.

      Compressor Type

      This air conditioner is fitted with a Rotary type of compressor, known for its superior performance and durability.

      Dust Filter

      Facilitated by a moisture removal mechanism, this brand of AC comes across as a hygienic air conditioner through the presence of an anti-bacterial filter called Koukin filter which works on the principle of Kaimim Function.

      Low Noise Level

      Capable of silent cooling, it comes as an easy means to toggle between varying fan speeds without bothering about the noise it generates.
