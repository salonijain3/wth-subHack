      3G Compatible

      As the Cain SBE comes with 3G compatibility, you can stay assured for high-speed internet and smooth video-calling. The laptop features a default 3G ultrastick; you simply need to insert a SIM card to get the fast-speed internet.

      Remarkable build of this 2-in-1 Cain SBE laptop with minimalistic design is yet another amazing feature! It sports a sleek body making it portable to carry around while work or study. Further, the laptop comes with 10-points Capacitive Touch Panel and IPS display to view images in best quality.

      Also, the Cain SBE features a Magnetic Cover along with Integrated Keyboard & Multi-touch Trackpad. Additionally, this will ensure safety of your notebook.

      2MP Primary & Secondary Camera

      Video chats will now be clearer as the Cain SBE 2-in-1 laptop is loaded with 2 MP front and rear camera. You can even take selfies with the front camera provided in the notebook.

      7900 mAh Li-Polymer Battery

      Packed with 7900 mAh Li-Polymer battery, the notebook promises quite strong battery backup. You can carry this device with you all day long and still don’t worry about battery low sign!

      A complete package of versatile features & smart look, the Cain Signature Black from Notion Ink is surely going to be your new computing companion. You can get this notebook only at Snapdeal!
