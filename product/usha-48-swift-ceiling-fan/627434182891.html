      Description

      Usha is one of the oldest and the most trusted brand for manufacturing fans in India. This claim is well supported by millions of loyal customers across the country. They are known to develop the most efficient products for the Indian environment especially small spaces at home or at work. The Usha Swift Ceiling Fan is the best fit for any such environment ensuring maximum air flow in crunched spaces. This 1200 MM sweep size wall fan is available in a beautiful White colour adding an element of richness to your décor at home as well as at office. It is highly energy efficient with its power consumption feature of less than 55 W. It has a maximum speed of 1280 RPM which is more than a regular ceiling fan delivering faster and uniform cooling throughout the room. This product comes with a unique oil reservoir lubricant unit for frictionless and soundless oscillation. The unique pivot arrangement makes it jerk free even in the most tight spaces.

       

      Design

      Usha is known for its sleek yet innovative designs. The Usha Swift Ceiling Fan is a brilliant testimonial of quality and creativity in the same product. It has a long lasting white paint with a metallic finish that adds an element of class to the office as well as your house. The powerful motor that delivers 350 RPM works best in ever-changing Indian climate. The unique auto –wound motor ensures durability beyond the warranty. One of the best feature of this product is the double ball bearing that provides frictionless and sound-free cooling.

       

      Efficient speed control

      The Usha Swift Ceiling Fan is a true example of convenience with high efficiency. You can now set the fan speed with the help of a regulator. A lower rotational speed can be set when the temperature is pleasant and you only want to promote circulation in the room. Higher fan speeds can be set when the temperature is soaring and you need instant cooling.

       

      Wide Air Delivery

      The 1200 MM wide sweep provides faster and uniform cooling especially in small spaces delivering 350 RPM

       

      Warranty

      The Usha Swift Ceiling Fan comes with a 1 year warranty. Though, Usha is amongst the most relied upon brand in the industry, a 1 year warranty offering ensures you to have stress-free sleep under the comfort of this cooling fan. It also ensures peaceful working at your workplace. It also ensures peaceful working at your workplace with a cooler mind under a cooler fan.
