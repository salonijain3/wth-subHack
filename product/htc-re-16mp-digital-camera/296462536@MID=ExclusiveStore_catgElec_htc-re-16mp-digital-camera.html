      Description

      RE - White by HTC on Sketchfab

      Relive! Reinvent! Rediscover! Reimagine! Reach the core of photographic possibilities with hTC RE digital camera. A fun and easy-to-use device, RE simply fits in the palm of your hand and captures crystal clear images with 16 Megapixel resolution. Take it underwater for candid selfies or fix it on the front of your bike, RE makes you stay in the moment with its super-easy one touch function. Viewfinders and Mode settings are now old-school. This hTC camera streams your photos and videos directly to your smart phone through hTC RE app. You can also use your smart phone as a shutter trigger and remote viewfinder for this digital camera.

      RE has a tube-like design and light weight body so that you can carry it around almost everywhere. You can record full HD 1080p videos at 30 fps with this camera and also capture slo-mo videos with striking details. Under its 146 degree viewing angle, nothing keeps out of your reach. RE is Wi-Fi direct and Bluetooth compatible for instant sharing. Its path-breaking technology and awe-aspiring features truly redefines the basics of photography.

      16 Megapixels Camera

      With a seamless design and periscope shaped body, hTC RE digital camera has brought a revolution in the field of professional photography. It comes with a 16 Megapixels 1/ 2.3 inch Sony sensor and f 2.8 lens that delivers amazing image results. You can instantly view the picture you have taken on your smartphone or use it as a live viewfinder to capture a brilliant photo with this camera.

      146 Degree Super-Wide Angle

      Good things come in small packages. hTC RE digital camera is a true-to-life example of this statement. This pocket-size invention is equipped to snap a super-wide angle photograph. It has a viewing angle of 146 degrees and it ensures that nothing remains out-of-frame when you take a click from it.

      Wi-Fi Direct & Bluetooth Enabled

      A distinctive feature of hTC RE digital camera that makes it more accessible is its Bluetooth and Wi-Fi Direct function. You can easily connect through other mobile phones through Bluetooth v4.0 and share music, videos and images. For super-fast data sharing, establish a direct peer to peer connection through Wi-Fi direct and connect with multiple smart phones in your area.  With Wi-Fi direct function, you can easily sync images and audio-visual data with many users simultaneously.

      Built-In Grip Sensor

      Gone are the days when you have to meticulously search for the right button to switch-on the camera. Simply grip hTC RE digital camera in your hand and the built-in sensor will switch it on. Operating a camera couldn’t get easier than this!

      One touch Function

      Want to click a picture? Just tap on the button on the top of hTC RE and it will capture a photo. No matter what your surroundings are, you can snap brilliant shots with this little gadget anywhere. If you are on a trek, strap it on your backpack and take images by a single touch on your smart phone. You can attach RE digital camera to the handle of your bike or your kid’s scooter to capture everyday moments instantly. This one touch function gives RE a special status and makes it stand apart from the crowd.

      Full HD Recording at 1080p

      You can record 1080p high definition videos at 30 frames per second with hTC RE digital camera. The superb quality offered by this camera makes you revisit the memories once more. It has a built-in microphone that records the live sound with minimized noise and enhances your movie viewing experience.

      Slo-Mo Video Function

      Be creative and explore your fun side with the slo-mo function of hTC RE. It can record time-lapse videos with 4X magnification at 720p and 30 frames per second with striking detail. Simply press the dedicated slo-mo button on the front of this digital camera and it gets ready to record slo-mo video. 

      Android and iOS Compatible with RE App

      This innovative gadget comes with an exclusive hTC RE app. You can install this app in any Android phone with Android 4.3 and above. RE digital camera is an also compatible iOS device and integrates seamlessly with your existing photo and video gallery. Once you have installed the app in your smart phone, you can control the functions of the camera through your phone easily. Your smart phone can act as a remote viewfinder and also a shutter trigger for the camera with RE app.

      Easy to Use

      hTC RE digital camera is the easiest and simplest gadget to take pictures and record videos. It can fit into your pocket and operates through a single touch button. You can strap it on your bag or clip it on your window sill to record high definition videos effortlessly. It is waterproof and you can take it into 1 metre water level for underwater photography. It weighs just 65.5 grams which makes it a perfect companion to carry while travelling. With 26.5mm diameter and 97.7mm height, it comes out to be so compact that it can fit into your pocket or can be put in a handbag quite easily.

      Memory Expansion through SD Card

      This digital camera comes with 32 MB ROM and 256 MB of DDR RAM. You can insert a micro SD card in your hTC RE digital camera to expand its memory. This digital camera is compatible with SDXC and SDHC storage formats which allow you to store a large amount of images and videos safely.

      Rechargeable Battery

      With 820 mAh rechargeable battery, you can continuously record FHD videos for 1 hour 50 minutes on your hTC RE digital camera. Once fully recharged, it can click up to 1200 images on an average.
