      Description

      If power cuts threaten to continuously plague your warm days and peaceful nights, then it’s time to invest in a reliable and efficient inverter. The Microtek UPSEB 1100 VA Inverter is a great choice if you wish to power up your home even in instances of power cuts. It is designed to match the looks of any contemporary home and offers an array of indicator displays that illuminate whenever a change in current is triggered. The smart inverter leverages a micro controller that helps it smartly identify a power cut or power restore and accordingly set itself into action. It is available at an amazing proce at Snapdeal. The inverter also comes with an assuring two-year warranty, which means that you do not have to worry about power cuts at all.

      Sleek design and latest technology

      While an inverter does take up a small space in your home, you would want it to be one that fits in perfectly, without affecting your home’s décor. The Microtek UPSEB 1100 VA Inverter has a sleek design that fits right into modern, contemporary homes. With its attractive indicator lights at play, you can view the status of your home or office power even from a distance. As it is in design, so it is in technology. This smart inverter employs a micro controller to help it make correct decisions, effectively. The DSP based design also helps it function flawlessly.

      Visible indicators

      The inverter’s front portion has the visual indicators arranged and laid out neatly. These help you identify when there is a power cut or when the inverter is charging. The indicators are extremely crucial when it comes to knowing whether your home is receiving power from the mains or from the inverter. It also lets you know when the battery charge of the inverter is running low. The display also lets you known when there is an overload on the inverter, which means that you are using more devices than your inverter can handle.

       

      Power specifications

      The Microtek UPSEB 1100 VA Inverter has a total capacity of 1000 VA. The operating frequency in the UPS Mode is around 50 Hz. The total output voltage in the UPS mode ranges between 200 Volts to 230 Volts. The inverter can conveniently power up your entire home without hindering your daily activities caused by constant power cuts.

      Short circuit protection

      You have the assurance of being protected even in cases of short circuit thanks to this safety feature incorporated in the Microtek UPSEB 1100 VA Inverter.
