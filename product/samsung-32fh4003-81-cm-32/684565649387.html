      Overview

      In order to upgrade your TV viewing experience and to make you feel like you are in a movie theatre, shop online for this Samsung 32FH4003 HD Ready LED Television at Snapdeal at a pocket-friendly price. It is stylish in terms of look and is engineered using advanced technology. This television promises to take TV viewing experience to a new height.

      Display Screen

      This Samsung LED TV has a sleek and sturdy design that will complement the decor of any modern living room. Its design ensures it can be easily mounted. You can now enjoy the stunning quality of pictures on the 80 cm LED display of this television in astonishing Full HD. Black in colour, it will complement all kinds of backgrounds. You will be able to enjoy vivid pictures as it comes with enhanced colour display and contrast.

      HD

      With a digital resolution of 1366 x 768 pixels, you are sure to enjoy unmatched clarity with the full HD display in true colours. It has an aspect ratio of 16:9. This Samsung 32FH4003 HD Ready LED Television offers a resolution that is substantially higher than that of standard definition television.

      Noise Filter

      Introducing-Samsung Clean View that makes your TV viewing more pleasurable. This feature offers auto noise removal technology that eliminates any kind of digital and analog noise, delivering crystal clear audio. The Analog Noise Filter of this feature is designed to enhance the quality of the picture, simply by deleting the visual noise from air and cable both.

      Best-in-class Durability

      Samsung 32FH4003 LED TV comprises of block capacitors that are responsible for the TV to absorb lightning strikes up to 15 KV. Additionally, to protect the TV from moisture, each chipset is coated with silica gel and anti-humidity glue. Moreover, the TV is equipped with varistor blocks and capacitors to survive 750V of high fluctuation. The TV has 1 year of free additional warranty on lightning, power surges and humidity.

      Wide Colour Enhancer Plus

      Often it has been noticed that the on-screen image looks faded. This happens because the TV has limited colour density. To eliminate this, the LED comes with Wide Colour Enhancer Plus feature that enhances the quality of the on-screen image, giving it a realistic look with more vibrant colours.

      USB Play

      Now play your favourite media content stored in your USB device with the help of the ConnectShare Movie feature. To use this, you simply need to plug your USB device in to the available slot in the TV and enjoy watching movies, photos and music without facing any hassles.

      Super Slim Panel

      This TV can be mounted on any wall or can be fixed on the stand according to your viewing comfort.

      Power Consumption

      The television requires an AC power supply of 110-240 V. It is extremely energy-efficient and eco-friendly.

      Connectivity Ports and other features

      This Samsung 32FH4003 HD Ready LED Television has one HDMI port, enabling you to connect your external storage devices to the TV and allows you to play your favourite content easily. Now you can also connect your pen drives or other USB drives directly to this TV as it has a USB port. It also had audio, analog and headphones output. It also features plug-and-play option so that you can simply connect your external device and can play movies, video tracks, audios and images on this wide screen. With VGA connectivity, you can turn this TV into a PC as well.
